Activity Monitor

The activity monitor allows you to see what people are doing in Proteus in real time.

Port

The activity monitor listens on the boadcast port sepcified in the configuration parameters.

You can override that port - to view the activity of another Proteus instance - by specifing the port on the URL. e.g.:
http://bulgroo.cs.umass.edu:1234/activity.html?port=2332
